# Data Models

## Keystatic Collections

### Careers

Defined in `keystatic.config.ts`:
- **Slug**: Unique job identifier.
- **Title**: Job title.
- **Intro**: Short role description.
- **Work Mode**: Remote, Hybrid, or On-site.
- **Location**: Physical city/country.
- **Employment Type**: Full-time, Part-time, or Contract.
- **Experience**: Required years of experience.
- **Display Order**: Controls sorting in UI.
- **Is Active**: Toggles visibility.
- **Role Overview**: Detailed document content.
- **Key Responsibilities**: Array of text.
- **Skills & Experience**: Array of text.
- **What Youll Bring**: Array of text.
- **Why Join Posilenz**: Detailed document content.

## Static Data

### Blog Post

Defined in `lib/blogData.ts`:
- **ID**: Unique ID.
- **Slug**: URL-friendly identifier.
- **Number**: Post sequence number.
- **Date**: Publication date.
- **Title**: Post title.
- **Image**: Featured image path.
- **Excerpt**: Short summary for list view.
- **Content**: HTML-based article content.
